Welcome to Job Page Draft Assistant

Job hunting is repetitive. This extension reduces the friction — scan a job posting, add your profile details, and get a tailored resume or cover letter draft without copying everything manually.

The extension is free. For real AI generation, connect your own API key in Settings. Demo Mode lets you try the full workflow with no key required.
